# StudentBridge – Project Plan

## 1. Project Overview

StudentBridge is a web-based platform designed to help international students in South Korea find part-time job opportunities during their studies. Many foreign students face difficulties finding suitable jobs due to language barriers, lack of local work experience, and limited information about available opportunities.

StudentBridge aims to bridge this gap by connecting international students with employers who are willing to hire student workers. The platform will provide a simple and accessible interface where students can search for jobs, apply directly, and build work experience while studying.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

International students often struggle to find part-time jobs because:

* Language barriers make communication difficult.
* Many employers prefer candidates with local work experience.
* Students have limited knowledge of available job opportunities.
* Existing job platforms are not designed specifically for international students.

These challenges make it difficult for students to support themselves financially and gain practical work experience while studying abroad.

---

## 3. Proposed Solution

StudentBridge will provide a centralized online platform specifically designed for international students in South Korea. The platform will allow students to create profiles, search for part-time job opportunities, and apply easily.

Employers will be able to post job listings targeted toward student workers and review applications through the system.

The platform will also include basic workplace language support to help students understand common Korean phrases used in part-time jobs.

---

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Users

* International students studying in South Korea
* Employers looking to hire student workers

### Secondary Users

* Universities that want to support student employment
* Local businesses near universities

---

## 5. Key Features

### Student Features

* User registration and login
* Create student profile
* Upload resume
* Search part-time jobs
* Apply for jobs
* Save favorite job postings
* Multi-language interface (English/Korean)

### Employer Features

* Employer registration and login
* Post part-time job listings
* Manage job postings
* View and review student applications
* Contact potential candidates

### Platform Features

* Job filtering by location and category
* Multi-language support
* Simple workplace language tips for students
* Notification system for new job postings

## 8. Monetization Strategy

StudentBridge can generate revenue through employer job postings. Employers may pay a small fee to post job listings or promote their job advertisements.

Additional revenue may come from:

* Featured job postings
* Employer subscription plans
* Recruitment services for companies seeking international talent
