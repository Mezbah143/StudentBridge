Issue Title
Project Idea Pitch – StudentBridge
Issue Description
## Project Idea

**StudentBridge** is a web platform designed to help international students in South Korea find part-time job opportunities more easily during their studies.

## Problem

Many international students struggle to find part-time jobs because of language barriers, lack of local work experience, and limited access to reliable job information. Existing job platforms are not specifically designed for international students.

## Proposed Solution

StudentBridge will provide a centralized platform where international students can search and apply for part-time jobs that are suitable for them. Employers can also post job opportunities targeted toward student workers.

## Key Features

- Student profile creation
- Resume upload
- Job search and filtering
- Job application system
- Employer job posting
- Multi-language support (Korean / English)

## Target Users

- International students studying in South Korea
- Employers looking to hire part-time student workers
